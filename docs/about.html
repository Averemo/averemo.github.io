About Averemo

We are a small group of people committed to being honest about topics of interest to the American voter. We love America and want to make it better for everyone.

What does "Averemo" mean? It is an invented word that is intended to mean "tell me the truth".

What is the audience for this website? We are trying to reach conservative voters that may currently support Donald Trump. This website should be a safe place for conservative voters to explore various topics without being insulted.

What is the editorial policy for this site?

  • Content should be true and verifiable with multiple references.
  • Everyone should be respected, regardless of their views.
  • If we make a mistake then we should correct the website.

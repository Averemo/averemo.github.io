Is DEI Good or Bad?

First, what is DEI? It stands for "Diversity, Equity and Inclusion".

Is it good or bad? The best judge of that is you. This quiz will explore various fictional scenarios involving DEI so that you can decide for yourself.

There are no right or wrong answers. This is just for you to discover your opinions. (Your answers are not saved anywhere.)

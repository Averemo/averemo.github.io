# Is the Iran War Good for America?

You can discuss this topic on [Discord](https://discord.com/channels/1429240627178110988/1490838283381375180).

On February 28, 2026, the United States and Israel launched a preemptive strike against Iran [\[Wikipedia\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2026_Iran_war).
The war is currently ongoing. So it is hard to know whether it will end up being good for America or not.
But we can look at the arguments for and against the war.

## Arguments For the War

* Demonstrates American might and resolve.
* Removed Ayatollah Khamenei and other top leaders from power by killing them. [\[Trump\]](https://truthsocial.com/@realDonaldTrump/posts/116150413051904167) [\[Just Security\]](https://www.justsecurity.org/135015/khamenei-killing-perilous-death-assassination-ban/)
* Might lead to a more democratic Iran. Iranian's celebrated the news. [\[IranIntl\]](https://www.iranintl.com/en/202602288551)
* Might lead to a more peaceful Middle East because Iran funds terrorist groups like Hezbollah, Hamas, and Houthis. [\[Wikipedia\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Axis_of_Resistance)

## Arguments Against the War

* It has resulted in the deaths of 13 Americans and 3531 Iranians as of April 3, 2026. [\[The Independent\]](https://www.the-independent.com/news/world/middle-east/iran-war-news-deaths-us-israel-april-3-b2951712.html)
* Other Iranian leaders took their place and they have even more reason to hate America. [\[Lawfare\]](https://www.lawfaremedia.org/article/understanding-iran-s-strategy-then--now--and-next)
* Trump was told in January of 2026 that an attack on Iran would not guarantee the collapse of the regime. [\[MSN\]](https://www.msn.com/en-us/news/world/trump-was-told-attack-on-iran-wouldn-t-guarantee-collapse-of-regime/ar-AA1Uincx)
* The Iran War has cost over $16 billion in the first 12 days. [\[CSIS\]](https://www.csis.org/analysis/iran-war-cost-estimate-update-113-billion-day-6-165-billion-day-12)
* It costs America much more to shoot down an Iranian drone than it costs Iran to launch one. [\[Politico\]](https://www.politico.com/news/2023/12/19/missile-drone-pentagon-houthi-attacks-iran-00132480)
* Our military stockpiles are being depleted and it will take years to replenish them. This makes us more vulnerable if we get into a war with Russia or China. [\[MSN\]](https://www.msn.com/en-ie/news/world/iran-s-drones-cost-35k-to-make-but-4m-to-shoot-down-as-western-missiles-are-weeks-from-running-out/ar-AA1XmjAZ?cvid=69a61176482a4b42a70a502eb2ded1d8)
* We have not captured their uranium so Iran could restart their nuclear program after the war. [\[PBS NewsHour\]](https://www.pbs.org/newshour/world/securing-irans-enriched-uranium-by-force-would-be-risky-and-complex-experts-say)
* The oil blockade has caused the price of oil to skyrocket, hurting the US economy as well as the rest of the world. As a result, American consumers are paying much more for gas and food. [\[JEC\]](https://www.jec.senate.gov/public/index.cfm/democrats/press-releases?ID=A4C3F903-BEE7-43EC-A3F4-EF73B0A485F5)
* Relations with NATO have been damaged because they were not consulted about the war. [\[CFR\]](https://www.cfr.org/articles/nato-is-marking-its-seventy-seventh-anniversary-will-it-be-its-last)
* Attacking Iran without an imminent threat is a violation of the UN Charter, which the US has signed. So President Trump is in violation of the Supremacy Clause of the US Constitution. [\[Justia\]](https://www.justia.com/international-law/treaties-under-international-and-us-law/)
* The global stock market has lost significant value since the war started. [\[Wikipedia\]](https://wikipedia.org/wiki/Economic_impact_of_the_2026_Iran_war)

## The Nuclear Issue

One of the most compelling arguments for war is that it may prevent Iran from developing nuclear weapons. Iran clearly does not like the US and Israel and the thought of Iran having nuclear weapons is terrifying.
One of the best possible outcomes would be if we could force Iran into a position where they would give up their nuclear program and allow international inspectors to verify that they are not developing nuclear weapons.

But we used to have a deal with Iran that prevented them from developing nuclear weapons. The agreement was negotiated by the Obama administration and was in place for several years.
[\[White House Archives\]](https://obamawhitehouse.archives.gov/the-press-office/2015/08/05/remarks-president-iran-nuclear-deal).

When Trump became President, he withdrew from the agreement and reimposed sanctions on Iran [\[BBC\]](https://www.bbc.com/news/world-us-canada-44045957).
This gave Iran a reason to restart their nuclear program.

At the time Obama said that Trump's decision to withdraw from the agreement could lead to war. [\[Newsweek\]](https://www.newsweek.com/iran-nuclear-deal-obama-trump-916037)

So all the lives lost, the money spent, and the loss of military might may only get us back to where we were before Trump first took office.
